Answers to common questions about Micro-Internships

Who can participate?

All students who are presently in high school or who have graduated high school in the last two years can participate.

What is the cost?

The cost is $99 per Micro-Internship module, and each student can decide how many modules to take.

What is the Isuriz value?

The Isuriz value is that you are interacting directly with professionals in a specific career that offers high quality at an affordable price point for a lot of people.
